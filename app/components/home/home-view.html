How it works

Grouplanner is as easy as one-two-three:

  • 1 Invite everybody
  • 2 Tap the days you are available
  • 3 Pick a date!

Why use Grouplanner?

Because it rocks! And we know the pain of keeping a group or team going. Using Grouplanner allows you to focus on what you're doing instead of when.
